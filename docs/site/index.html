About

All you need is a ledger — an accurate and good one. Even better if that ledger is also an MCP server.

We provide our ledger for you freely. If you are not satisfied with our risk management you can simply add the trades, the stop-loss fractions and the profit fractions if SL/TP is desired, and then calculate how much profit your trades made.

Brokers usually keep track of that for you — but what if your broker goes down? So do your trades and potentially your assets. Our ledger takes care of the trade entries.

What if you are trading physical assets that you cannot trade on a broker? Gold, real estate, private deals, tokens on a DEX with no portfolio view?

Quant Ledger is a single source of truth across every asset class. Record the buy, set your exits, watch the math, and own your data — no API keys, no broker lock-in, no subscriptions.

What you get

▶
Trade Journal
Record buys and sells with price, quantity, fees, and timestamps. Import historical trades or execute new ones that move your wallet balance.
▲
Exit Strategies
Attach take-profit and stop-loss levels to every position. Set multiple exit points per trade — partial sells at different targets. Toggle stop-losses on or off as the market moves.
★
Profit & Loss
Instant P&L calculation for any trade at any price. Net profit, gross profit, ROI — with fee deductions built in.
⚙
Serial Generator
Model compound-growth strategies: define an entry price, overhead, exit fraction, and steepness — generate a full plan of scaled entries with calculated take-profits and sell quantities.
♦
Entry Points
Plan where to enter the market before you commit capital. Store target prices, quantities, and risk parameters. Feed entries into managed chains for systematic execution.
⛓
Managed Chains
Group entries into cycles. Activate a chain, add entries to each cycle, track savings rate and reinvestment. The chain manager handles the bookkeeping of compounding strategies.
☷
Overhead Calculator
Compute the real cost of a trade: spreads, hedging fees, time decay, and opportunity cost across your portfolio. Know your breakeven before you click buy.
⚡
MCP Daemon
The app runs a built-in MCP server on your device. Connect Claude, Copilot, or any AI assistant over your local network and let them inspect your portfolio, run what-if analyses, generate plans, and execute trades — all with confirmation prompts. Desktop and Android.

Workflow

1.Fund your wallet — deposit your starting capital so the ledger can track balances.
2.Plan entries — use the Serial Generator to model a strategy, or add entry points manually.
3.Execute or import trades — Execute Buy debits your wallet; Import Buy records without moving funds.
4.Set exits — attach TP/SL levels to each buy. Partial sells at different price targets.
5.Close positions — Execute Sell credits your wallet; the ledger calculates realised P&L automatically.
6.Repeat & compound — use managed chains to roll profits into the next cycle with a savings rate.

Philosophy

Quant Ledger is open source and runs entirely on your device. There is no account, no telemetry, no cloud requirement. Your trades are your business.

The risk management tools — serial plans, overhead calculations, chain compounding — exist because trading is not just about entries. Knowing when to sell, how much to sell, and what it really costs is what separates a journal from a strategy.

We don’t connect to brokers, don’t stream prices, and don’t offer signals. This is a calculator and a notebook — you bring the decisions.
